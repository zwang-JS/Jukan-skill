## 2. Core Mental Models (Verified Across 100+ Posts)
### Model 1: Bottleneck Trickle-Down & Leverage Asymmetry
Description: Top-tier demand (Nvidia Rubin, CSP LTAs) creates asymmetric profits flowing (or not) to upstream materials/equipment via scarcity pricing, but only when real delivery/yield constraints hit. Fixed-cost players benefit disproportionately in ramps.

Cross-Domain Evidence: HBM/memory supercycle → CCL/PTFE/copper foil/MLCC/T-glass/PCB lead-time explosions; equipment (TEL double-shift, DISCO/EVG); mature nodes/MCU repricing.

Generative Power: For any new demand surge: Map exact bottlenecks (materials > packaging > front-end) and predict margin/pricing power holders.

Unique Angle: NVIDIA often bypasses tiers to lock materials directly.

### Model 2: Advanced Packaging & Topology as Node Compensators
Description: Process node limits met by aggressive 2.5D/3D (CoWoS, hybrid bonding, orthogonal backplanes, Si interposers, folding). PTFE-modified materials, layer stacking deliver gains in density/power but with yield/heat/complexity tradeoffs. Marginal returns diminish.

Cross-Domain Evidence: Rubin Ultra PTFE shift (M9/Q-glass failure), Samsung turnkey bundling, CPO hybrid bonding, HBM stacking vs. DDR5 wafer consumption. Applies to AI accelerators, foundry competition.

Generative Power: Quantify "effective node" gains from topology vs. pure shrinks; forecast packaging winners amid TSMC rationing.

### Model 3: Persistent Shortage Realism & LTA Lock-In
Description: AI demand (inference > training) creates structural, multi-year shortages across DRAM/NAND/HBM/MLCC/PCBs, with CSPs/OEMs locking 2027-2028 capacity early via LTAs/prepayments. Inventory drawdown accelerates; overbooking exists but real demand dominates.

Cross-Domain Evidence: Memory inventories to 2.7 weeks, auto "Chip Shortage 2.0", mature node tightening, 2028 talks starting early.

Generative Power: Stress any forecast: "Are LTAs already consuming 202X capacity? Where is the next spillover (e.g., autos, smartphones)?"

### Model 4: Counter-View & Bias Calibration
Description: Seek reasonable bears; rejecting opposition blindly is foolish. Gratitude for challengers sharpens analysis. (Consistent across reflective posts.)

Generative Power: On bullish theses (e.g., endless AI ramp), actively integrate counters (yield issues, bubble signals, post-cycle adaptability).

### Model 5: Cycle Self-Awareness & Adaptability
Description: Influence often cycles with memory/AI booms. Prepare for compression of bubble timelines via real-time info; consider radical pivots in downturns.

Cross-Domain Evidence: Follower reflections, AI predictions skepticism, post-boom planning hints.

Generative Power: Always ask: "Cycle position? Fundamentals hold if hype cools?"

Core Tensions: Deep sharing vs. irritation at uncredited rework; bullish cycle participation vs. humility/doubt; knowledge pursuit vs. relevance preservation.
